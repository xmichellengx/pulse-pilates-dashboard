# Pulse Pilates — Business Profile

## Overview
Pulse Pilates (pilatesreformer.my) is a Malaysian pilates reformer equipment retailer and rental company. They source equipment from Chinese manufacturers and sell/rent under the Pulse Pilates brand, including custom-colour and logo-engraved orders for studios (Pulse for Business / P4B).

## Sales Modes
1. **Direct Purchase** — full payment or CC installment (Atome, bank transfer, CC terminal)
2. **Rental** — minimum 3 months, auto-debit via Payex
   - Alu II Reformer: RM 390/month
   - Alu II Foldable: RM 450/month
   - Alu II Tower: RM 590/month
   - Delivery RM 150–300 + Installation RM 80 upfront
3. **Rental → Purchase conversion** — up to 2 months rental credit rebate
4. **Pulse for Business (P4B)** — B2B studio packages, 40–50% booking fee, custom colours/logos

## Sales SOP (5 Steps)
1. **Inquiry** — Leads logged in tracker, replied via Chatdaddy with saved templates
2. **Closing** — Order form sent via WhatsApp, payment collected, submitted to Sales + Accounts WA group
3. **Delivery** — Posted to WhatsApp "Trip" group; KV: Tue–Thu; outer states: within 2 weeks
4. **Admin** — Invoice generated via Google Forms, emailed as PDF
5. **Rental follow-up** — Calendar invites at 1, 2, 3 months to push for purchase conversion

### Delivery Charges
- Klang Valley / Selangor: RM 150/trip
- Outer states: RM 250/trip
- Installation: RM 80/reformer, RM 150/cadillac
